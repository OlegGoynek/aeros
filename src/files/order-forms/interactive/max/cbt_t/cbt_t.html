About this order-form

We hope this order form will ease the process of choosing colors and materials, so you could order exactly the glider you want.

Colors and shapes

Please keep in mind, though: colors that you see on the display do not exactly match the colors of materials! The structure of cloth is not represented at all. Transparency and colors of materials in reality also depend on lighting conditions.
This order form contains the sketch, that should only give you an idea about how the whole assembly will look like.
Trike and wing representation is very approximate. On trike - only colors are changing. Engine assembly, pylon fairing, saddle bags and other options do not affect the picture.

Combat T and ANT trike options

You can use this form to order both wing and trike, or only the wing - select respective order option. If you order only the wing, trike colors are still available for selecting in form - that is just to make a complete picture.
* We install an engine kill switch with rescue system. If you decide to use your own rescue system, you may order a kill switch separately. In that case please mention it in 'notes' section along with required cable length.

Filling the form and Placing the order

You can choose most of colors by clicking on the wing panel or trike part. Colors of ribs and batten pockets, and all other options are available via form menu. When ready, click the DOWNLOAD PDF button on the bottom of page. A .pdf file will open in a new tab/window or you will be prompted to save/open the file.
Please, check the order form thoroughly and send it to your dealer or to info@aeros.com.ua.
