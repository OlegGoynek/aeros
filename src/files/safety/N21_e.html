Safety bulletin N 21 from 20/10/2015

The lever batten tips used in Aeros hang gliders and trike wings Profi TL, Profi and Still are manufactured by Airborne Australia. Airborne has posted a service advisory regarding the lever tips:

http://www.airborne.com.au/images/aw_directives/SB-019.pdf

The advisory addresses adjustment, maintenance and inspection issues with regard to the lever tips. There have been incidents recently in which lever tips opening in flight have compromised the pilot’s control of the glider. Aeros has reviewed the contents of the advisory, and we agree with Airborne’s recommendations. We encourage the pilots flying Aeros hang gliders and trike wings equipped with lever tips to read the advisory.
