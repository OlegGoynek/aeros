Safety bulletin N 10 from 28/04/2011
Attention pilots flying Aeros hang gliders

We have become aware of a potential problem with the Aeros Combat and Discus hang gliders. The pulley cap screw (marked red on the photo below) was loosened on the VG pulleys.

We strongly recommend you to unscrew cap screws from all pulleys on your gliders, and screw them back using a Loctite compound.

Also, refer to the safety bulletin #7 from 19.05.2009 and check the following items:

  • sweep wire condition along its entire length;
  • sprog angles.

Make surre the sweep wire shackle is bolted and a safety nut is tighten.

Fly safe!

Yours, Team Aeros
