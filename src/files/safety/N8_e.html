Safety bulletin N8 from 01/06/2009

Dear owners of Viper-S harnesses,

Due to resent report of the problems with Viper harnesses we would like you to check few items on your harnesses (see the picture below):
1. Make sure the pulley of pitchrope has no sharp edges, also the hole in carbon plate, where the rope goes through, it can damages the rope;
2. The rubber of the clutch may get damaged by too sharp edges in the carbon plate (it is already repaired with newer models);
3. Make sure the pins for the steel slider rod has safety rings.

We wish you happy flights and safe landings.

Aeros team
