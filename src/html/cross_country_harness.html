Details

The Aeros Cross Country Harness is designed for pilots flying for their pleasure above all. It has power frame made of tubes, parachute and some other pockets located on the outside of the harness instead of the inside. This harness has tons of storage room. Inside there is a full-length glider storage bag as well as a camelback pocket, wallet pocket, change pocket and lash down points for your oxygen system.
