Details

If you're looking for a world class competition or cross country harness, look no further. We would like to represent our new Viper-S harness which is the next level in aerodynamic performance and comfort. The Viper-S is without a doubt the lowest drag harness made by Aeros factory. The Aeros design team was able to utilize the Antonov wind tunnel for in-depth study and application of various harness drag reduction ideas. The result is a complete makeover of the Aeros racing harness design.

It retains the best features of Aeros current Viper harnesses and combines them into a design that offers a super clean aerodynamic profile, reduced frontal area, and increased storage. The Aeros Viper-S will provide you with competitive performance and superior comfort, whatever the duration of your flight.

The Viper — S same as previous Viper harnesses is utilize a carbon fiber backplate. This 330 mm(1.08 ft) wide, gently curving back support system allows the harness to fit as close as possible to the pilot while still allowing 'non-binding' support. Depending on the pilot's height, the backplate is between 800 mm (2.62ft) - 1050 mm (3.44ft) long.

The Viper-S has an optional Matrix cloth outer skin for drag reduction. This cloth has been proved in the wind tunnel to be the most 'slippery' available at the moment. With the Viper-S, same as with all Viper series you are able to replace the entire outer matrix cloth covering at anytime with a new one.

Employing a lever system which is more reliable than those used in previous models, the Viper-S pilot is able to easily adjust the pitch (the angle of attack) of the harness in flight with his butt-without taking hands off the basebar.

Distinctive Features:

Pockets for pilot's elbows fairing;

Reduced weight of the harness;

Increased reliability and lifetime of the trim device;

More streamlined shape of the harness's tail part;

New design of the inner side pockets.
