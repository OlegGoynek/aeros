Details

This model was designed with a more simple and less expensive multipurpose trike in mind.

The "Aeros Cross Country" trike will interest many customers as a trike with extended functional capabilities for use in various areas, under various climatic and geophysical conditions.

Modifications

You can order the "Aeros 2" trike in any form, from a kit to the ready-to-fly trike, and of course there are many options for you to choose from.

The "Aeros 2" can be equipped with the engine, wing and instruments of your choice, floats or skis, rescue system, etc. On the trikes "general information" page we've tried to publish comprehensive information on making a trike with ultimate consideration of the customer's opportunities and wishes.

Wings for every taste

The trike wings that are produced by AEROS (Stranger, Stream, Still, Profi, StingRay) have firmly taken their place in the ultralight market and have made an excellent name for themselves in all possible applications. Click on the wings' names to receive information about them and to choose a wing to your liking.

Wheels for various landing conditions

The main landing gear of pyramidal shape combined with automobile shock absorbers makes it possible to fly from and land on unimproved fields, increases safety on rough landings, makes steering much more comfortable. A trike with skies or floats is an option as well.
